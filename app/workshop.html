GreenHub

A collaborative approach to power consumption analysis of mobile devices

Hugo Matalonga

20th January 2017, University of Beira Interior

GreenHub is...

A platform, a mobile app, a collaborative initiave...

To the power consumption analysis of mobile devices.

Why?

Our goal is to build an open repository of data, collected through the mobile app.

So that everyone can use the data for research promoting collaboration between the community.
